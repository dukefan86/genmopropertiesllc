Edward is a South Carolina native who graduated from Clover High School in 2004. He then joined the Marine Corps in 2006 as a Communication Electronic Maintenance Technician. He has deployed a total of 3 times during his 15 year career, including Iraq and Afghanistan. Edward met his wife Andrea and married in November of 2013. He has 2 sons, Andrew and Charlie who are both in elementary school. Upon leaving the Marine Corps in 2021 he and his family moved back to Clover, SC where they currently reside. Edward is currently a full time student at the University of South Carolina majoring in Integrated Information Technology and will be graduating in May 2025.

Andrea was born in Ecuador and moved to the United States in 2006. She then joined the Marine Corps in 2008 as a Supply Administration Clerk. During her time in the marine Corps she deployed to Afghanistan in 2011. Upon leaving active duty she met Edward in 2013 and got married in November. She attended The Ohio State University and obtained her Master degree in Applied Clinical Research. She is currently working full time as a Patient Recruitment Specialist with Pfizer while still being a reservist in the Marine Corps.
